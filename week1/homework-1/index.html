After Life

After Life is a British dark comedy-drama about Tony, a man who becomes bitter and reckless after his wife's death. As he struggles with grief, his friends try to help him find meaning in life again.

Episodes

Episode 1

Tony conducts an interview with a terminally ill child, which profoundly affects him. Subsequently, he decides on the allocation of Lisa's insurance money and participates in the town fair with his friends.

Episode 2

Following another personal loss, Tony requests solitude from his friends and colleagues. He later contemplates the process of moving forward with his life

Episode 3

In an effort to find happiness, or at least reduce his misery, Tony acknowledges and appreciates those who have supported him throughout his struggles.

Episode 4

Anne offers Tony guidance on progressing with his life. Emma encounters an old acquaintance, Matt experiences a health scare, and James asserts himself.

Episode 5

Brian persuades Tony and the team to visit his apartment for a story. Meanwhile, Matt receives an alarming call from his son's school. EPISODE.NINJA

Cast

Ricky Gervais

The main character, a journalist struggling with grief after his wife's death, coping with life's challenges through sarcasm and dark humor.

Diane Morgan

Tony's supportive and caring friend, who often tries to help him move forward and find purpose despite his bitterness.

Ashley Jensen

A fellow employee at the local newspaper, offering comic relief while dealing with her own struggles in the workplace.

Mandeep Dhillon

A compassionate and hardworking member of the office staff, who is often caught in awkward situations but still cares deeply for Tony.
